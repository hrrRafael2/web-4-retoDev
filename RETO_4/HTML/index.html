Classic Cheesecake Recipe.

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired
With a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can deny its simple decadence.
For the best results, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

    Graham Crackers Crust

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs(about 10 full sheet graham crackers)
  • 5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

    Graham Crackers Crust

  • four 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240g) full-fat sour cream, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • topping suggestions: salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry topping, chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or raspberry sauce (recipe in notes)

  • In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, butter, and sugar.
  • Using a pastry brush, lightly coat the bottom of the prepared baking sheet with the graham cracker mixture.
  • Bake for 15 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
  • Remove the baking sheet from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes.
  • In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, granulated sugar, sour cream, and vanilla.
  • Pour the mixture into the prepared baking sheet.
